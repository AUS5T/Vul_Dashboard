Known Exploited Vulnerabilities

This dashboard provides information about known exploited vulnerabilities (KEV) tracked by CISA. It pulls data from multiple sources including the National Vulnerability Database (NVD), the Known Exploited Vulnerabilities catalog, and EPSS (Exploit Prediction Scoring System).

Each entry is enriched with key details such as CVSS severity scores, EPSS exploitation likelihood, required remediation actions, and due dates. The goal is to quickly prioritize and respond to threats that are actively being exploited in the wild.
